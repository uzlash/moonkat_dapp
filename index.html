FEATURES

Community Built

We locked 75% of the total supply on Pancake Swap. To ensure a fair and complete distribution, the MoonKat team owns 5% of all tokens which is cliffed for 6 months. For the first 6 months everyone has to buy on the open market.

Anti Pump-Dump-Exit Whales

Transactions (sell/buy) that trade more than 0.05% of the total supply will be rejected. Whales who make a transfer (between 2 wallets) that is larger than 0.05% of the total supply will be charged for 2.5 BNB.

Automatic Liquidity Pool

2% of every transaction automatically adds to the liquidity on Pancake Swap. Benefiting long term for $MKAT holders the most!

EARN $BNB REWARDS

3% of every transaction is taken and re-distributed to moonkat holders in $BNB. $MKAT holders can claim their BNB at the end of their weekly cycle.

No Locked Staking

Users don't have to lock up their tokens to claim their rewards, just hold MKAT to claim BNB weekly from DApp
